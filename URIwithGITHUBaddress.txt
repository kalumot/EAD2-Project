URI SCHEME
- https://ead2ca2api.azurewebsites.net/api/team/{team.code}    (Gives all the matches stored that the team are involved in)
- https://ead2ca2api.azurewebsites.net/api/team/all   (Shows all the teams)
- https://ead2ca2api.azurewebsites.net/api/match/all   (Shows the last 5 matches added no matter the team)
